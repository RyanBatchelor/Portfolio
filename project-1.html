Project Overview

279 Construction Supplies is what I wish I could build for my current company. We have a shipping and recieving department that takes in orders from construction crews and delivers supplies to the crews out in the field. They are an internal Amazon for the comapny so I made them a page that resembles Amazon.

The frontend was created using React, Redux, Bootstrap and Sass. The backend was created with javascript, express, JWT and all data is stored in MangoDB. The users can create accounts which will let them add items to their cart and checkout. Users may also leave reviews for the different items. Admin accounts can view a full list of orders and can mark orders as shipped. Admins can also add new products, manage inventory and user accounts.
